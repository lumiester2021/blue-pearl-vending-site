FAQs

Common questions before placing a vending machine.

Most questions come down to fit, service, and communication. This page answers the basics so property and operations teams can evaluate whether Blue Pearl is the right local partner.

What locations are a good fit?

Warehouses, offices, apartment communities, coworking spaces, gyms, and commercial properties with consistent traffic are usually the best fit.

Do you handle all stocking and service?

Yes. Blue Pearl handles placement, restocking, servicing, and adjustments to the product mix over time.

Can the machine accept cards and mobile payments?

Yes. Cashless payment is part of the service because it improves convenience and daily usage.

Can the product selection be customized?

Yes. The mix can be tailored around the location, audience, and what actually sells on-site.

Which areas do you serve?

Blue Pearl is focused on Virginia Beach, Norfolk, Chesapeake, and Hampton, with service across the greater Hampton Roads area.

How do we start the process?

Reach out through the contact page with the site type, city, and approximate number of users. Blue Pearl can then review whether the location is a strong fit.
